our teams

Electrical Team

The electrical team was in charge of speed and throttle control of the motor, design of electric vehicle charging system, PCB Design,and User interaction system design.
The team is currently working on two papers regarding Battery and Motor Sizing for E-bikes and E-tricycles, and Speed and Motor Control for BLDC Motors for E-bikes and E-Tricycles.

Mechanical Team

The mechanical team was tasked with design of the chassis, modification of the vehicle body, drive train design and implementation, structural and strength analysis of the system.
The team is fluent in 3D modeling, structural simulation, and the machining of components.

Navigation Team

The navigation team is in charge of developing the autonomous driving system for the tractor. This involved implementation of systems for object detection, obstacle avoidance and GPS guidance.
The team is currently working on implementing self-tilling capabilities in the tractor bot.
